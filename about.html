About us

MFMS is the build by the 4th sem students as project work as well as for the better management of the materials provided by the university and keeping track of the material which can be benefial for both students and administrative It is build with mission of creating user friendly environment within the university.

Vision of the Materials flow management system

  • Admin is provided with administrative functions such as adding or removing materials from the catalog and generating reports.
  • system facilitate the borrowing and returning of materials by keeping track of materials and the availability of items.
  • Students are able to see which materials that they borrowed.
  • Students are able to transfer the materials to another fellow.
